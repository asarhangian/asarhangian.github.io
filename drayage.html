Drayage Checklist

Keep TRUCRS clean, hit visit minimums, and plan equipment turnover early.

Account & registry

  • TRUCRS account verified; all units listed (correct VINs)
  • Primary contact email/phone up to date; backup authorized

Unit status

  • Each tractor labeled: ZE, low-NOx, or legacy
  • Legacy units: useful-life exit date planned
  • Open recalls & PM up to date

Visit tracking

  • Annual port visit minimum met (gate transactions)
  • Monthly report exported & archived
  • Exceptions documented (shop/out-of-service)

Incentives & funding

  • HVIP eligibility checked; voucher windows tracked
  • Port Clean Truck Fund (CTF) alignment noted
  • Infrastructure partner identified (if ZE)

Need templates? Grab them on the California page.

AB5 readiness (if using contractors)

  • Engagement model reviewed with counsel
  • Contracts updated; no lease-back traps
  • Policy & SOPs prepped for audits
